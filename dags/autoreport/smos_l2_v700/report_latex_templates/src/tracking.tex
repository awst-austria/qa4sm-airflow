\section{Performance Tracking}

The performance of SMOS L2 is tracked against the two reference datasets (ERA5-Land and C3S combined)
in terms of ubRMSD and Pearson's R. Performance tracking considers results from the latest 12 validation epochs.
To ensure samples that are large enough to compute the metrics, data from the last 3 months is used
(via a rolling window), meaning that there is a 2-month overlap between adjacent epochs.

Figure \ref{fig:tracking_R} tracks the performance of SMOS L2 in terms of Pearson's R against ERA5-Land
(Fig. \ref{fig:tracking_R}a) and C3S COMBINED (Fig. \ref{fig:tracking_R}b).
